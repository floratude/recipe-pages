Mediterranean Chicken Salad

Your new go-to salad bowl: Mediterranean Chicken Salad recipe for you! Juicy chicken, aromatic fresh veggies, and a burst of flavor in every bite—packed from top to bottom!

Ingredients

  • Lehsan paste (Garlic paste) 1 tsp
  • Mustard paste 1 tsp (Garlic paste) 1 tsp
  • Paprika powder ½ tsp
  • Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed ½ tsp
  • Dried oregano 1 tsp
  • Himalayan pink salt ½ tsp or to taste
  • Lemon juice 1 tbs
  • Olive oil 1 tbs
  • Chicken fillet 400g
  • Cooking oil 1 tsp
  • Iceberg lettuce shredded 2 Cups
  • Green olives ½ Cup
  • Yellow bell pepper julienne 1 large
  • Cherry tomatoes 1 Cup
  • Kheera (Cucumber) 2 medium
  • Pyaz (Onion) 1 medium
  • Extra virgin olive oil 3 tbs
  • Lemon juice 2 tbs
  • Apple cider vinegar 1 & ½ tbs
  • Dried oregano 1 tsp
  • Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed ½ tsp
  • Dried basil leaves 1 tsp
  • Himalayan pink salt ½ tsp or to taste
  • Mustard paste 2 tsp
  • Lehsan (Garlic) chopped 1 tsp
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped handful
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, add garlic paste, mustard paste, paprika powder, black pepper crushed, dried oregano, pink salt, lemon juice, olive oil & mix well.
  2. Add chicken fillet & coat well, cover & marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. In a frying pan, add cooking oil, marinated chicken fillet & cook on medium flame from both sides until done & apply remaining marinade in between.
  4. Let it rest for few minutes then cut into slices & set aside.
  5. In a bowl, add iceberg lettuce, green olives, yellow bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, onion.
  6. In a jar, add olive oil, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, dried oregano, black pepper crushed, dried basil leaves, pink salt, mustard paste, garlic, cover & shake well. Salad dressing is ready!
  7. Pour on vegetables, add fresh coriander & give it a good mix.
  8. Add prepared chicken slices, lemon wedges & serve!
